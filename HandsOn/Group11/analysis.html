Defining our Resource Naming Strategy

First of all, since our datasets are small, we are going to use hash URIs.
The URI domain that we are going to use is:

    http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/

In our ontology, we will have different forms for our classes, properties and instances. These forms are:

  • Classes:
    http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/ontology/<ontologyName>#<className>

  • Properties:
    http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/ontology/<ontologyName>#<propertyName>

  • Instances:
    http://safemadrid.linkeddata.es/resource/<className>/<identifier>
