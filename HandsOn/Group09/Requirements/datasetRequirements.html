datasetRequirements

  • R1. Data are in one of the selected smart city domains.
  • The dataset was found on the Madrid’s city hall website, which belongs to one of the domains selected.
  • R2. Data are available as a CSV file.
  • Our dataset has been downloaded as a CSV file although it's also available as an Excel document.
  • R3. Data have an open license so they can be published.
  • We have checked and it says that the dataset may be reused for commercial and noncommercial purposes.  

License: https://datos.madrid.es/egob/catalogo/aviso-legal

  • R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities (e.g., locations).
  • Each entry available in the csv file has a location: street, street number, district
